Hi there! 😃

My name is Weizheng Lu (鲁蔚征 in Chinese). 

I am a research engineer at High-Performance Computing Center, Renmin University of China. I am focusing on computing techniques at the intersection of `AI x Social Science`. I'm broadly interested in Machine Learning System, Big Data, Data Management and Data Science Workflows.

I am also a technical writer. I have wroten posts and tutorials on various topics including Flink, GPU and Deep Learning. All these posts are published on my Chinese [website](https://lulaoshi.info/).

Previously, I was a big data engineer at [Xiaomi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Xiaomi). I developed the recommendation system of Xiaomi Browser. I had also worked at [Zhipin](https://www.zhipin.com/), the largest online recruitment platform in China. 

I received my master's degree at Peking University and my bachelor's degree at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications.
